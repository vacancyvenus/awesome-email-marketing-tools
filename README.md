## All-in-One Platforms

- **[ActiveCampaign](https://emailtoolspot.com/tools/activecampaign/)** — CRM + email marketing + automation. Strong for small to mid-size businesses.
- **[HubSpot](https://emailtoolspot.com/tools/hubspot/)** — Full marketing hub with email, CRM, and sales tools. Scales with your business.
- **[Klaviyo](https://emailtoolspot.com/tools/klaviyo/)** — Ecommerce-focused email and SMS platform. Built for online stores.
- **[Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)](https://emailtoolspot.com/tools/brevo/)** — Affordable all-in-one with email, SMS, chat, and CRM.
- **[Mailchimp](https://emailtoolspot.com/tools/mailchimp/)** — The classic all-in-one marketing platform. Good for beginners.
- **[Drip](https://emailtoolspot.com/tools/drip/)** — Ecommerce CRM with powerful automation workflows.
- **[GetResponse](https://emailtoolspot.com/tools/getresponse/)** — Email marketing with webinar, funnel, and automation features.
- **[Omnisend](https://emailtoolspot.com/tools/omnisend/)** — Ecommerce omnichannel marketing automation.
- **[SendGrid](https://emailtoolspot.com/tools/sendgrid/)** — Cloud-based email delivery for developers and businesses.
- **[MailerLite](https://emailtoolspot.com/tools/mailerlite/)** — Simple, affordable email marketing for creators and small businesses.
